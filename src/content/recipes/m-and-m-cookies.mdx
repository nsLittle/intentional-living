text: "My M&M Cookie mix is a celebration of sweet, nostalgic flavors and endless versatility, perfect for every holiday and special occasion. The soft, chewy cookie base is a delightful canvas, made even more festive with the bright crunch of classic M&M candies.

This mix is elevated by a carefully balanced blend of vanilla and brown sugar, creating a rich, buttery dough that bakes to perfection every time. Each bite delivers a burst of sweetness and a playful pop of color, making these cookies a treat for both the eyes and the taste buds.Designed for versatility, this mix invites your creativity. Swap out the M&Ms for seasonal sprinkles, chocolate chips, or crushed peppermint for a personalized holiday twist. Whether you’re baking for a cozy evening in or sharing treats with loved ones, our M&M Cookie mix brings joy and sweetness to every celebration."
yield: "24 Cookies"

prep time: "10 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/2 cup flour"
  - "1 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/2 teaspoon baking soda"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "1 cup M&Ms, or other add-ins"
  - "1/3 cup dark brown sugar"
  - "1/2 cup granulated sugar"
steps:
  - "Measure and layer the ingredients in order."

bakingPrepTime: "15 minutes"
bakingTime: "20 minutes"
bakingIngredients:
  - "1 Jar M&M Cookie mix"
  - "1 egg"
  - "2 Tablespoons milk"
  - "1 teaspoon vanilla"
bakingSteps:
  - "Preheat oven to 350°."
  - "Mix M&M cookie mix with the ingredients above."
  - "Drop spoonfuls onto prepared cookie sheets."
  - "Bake for 10 minutes, or until golden. Repeat."
