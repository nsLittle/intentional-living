Whether you’re baking for a special occasion, a cozy night in, or just because, this jar makes it easy to whip up a batch of decadent, homemade brownies without the fuss. All you need are a few simple pantry staples to transform this mix into a tray of rich, chocolatey goodness."
yield: "24 Brownie Bars"

prep time: "10 minutes"
ingredients:
  - "1 1/3 cups granulated sugar"
  - "3/4 cup baking cocoa powder"
  - "1 1/3 cups flour"
  - "1/2 teaspoon baking powder"
  - "1/4 teaspoon salt"
  - "3/4 cup chocolate chips"

steps:
  - "Measure and layer the ingredients in order."

bakingPrepTime: "10 minutes"
bakingTime: "25 minutes"
bakingIngredients:
  - "1 Simple Jars Ultimate Brownie mix"
  - "3/4 cup melted butter"
  - "2 eggs"
  - "2 Tablespoons water"
  - "2 teaspoons vanilla"

bakingSteps:
  - "Preheat oven to 350°."
  - "Prepare 9 x 13″ baking pan."
  - "Mix all ingredients together in large bowl."
  - "Pour mixture into prepare pan."
  - "Bake for 18-25 minutes."
  - "Dust with powdered sugar and cut into squares after cooling. Enjoy!"
